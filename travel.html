Visa Information

Indonesia offers various visa options for international visitors:
  • Visa-free entry (for eligible countries)
  • Visa on Arrival (VOA) - 30 days, extendable once
  • E-visa application before travel

Visa Requirements

Check your visa requirements on the Indonesian Immigration website.

For visa support letters, please contact: icot2025@ideas-lab.org

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months
  • Proof of onward/return travel may be required
  • Visa regulations may change - check official sources

Conference Venue

Universitas Kristen Maranatha

The 13th International Conference on Orange Technology (ICOT 2025) will be held at:

Address: Universitas Kristen Maranatha, Jl. Prof. drg. Surya Sumantri No.65, Sukawarna, Kec. Sukajadi, Kota Bandung, Jawa Barat 40164, Indonesia

About the Venue

Universitas Kristen Maranatha is one of Indonesia's premier private Christian universities, located in the heart of Bandung city. Founded in 1965, the university has established itself as a leading institution for higher education in Indonesia.

The university's modern facilities and strategic location make it an ideal venue for international conferences, with state-of-the-art conference halls and advanced technological infrastructure.

The 13th International Conference on Orange Technology

A premier forum for researchers, engineers, and practitioners to present and discuss the latest research, innovations, and applications in the field of Orange Technology.
