About Universitas Kristen Maranatha

Universitas Kristen Maranatha is one of Indonesia's premier private Christian universities, located in the heart of Bandung city. Founded in 1965, the university has established itself as a leading institution for higher education in Indonesia, consistently delivering excellence in academic programs and research.

The university's modern facilities and strategic location make it an ideal venue for international conferences. With state-of-the-art conference halls, advanced technological infrastructure, and a beautiful campus environment, Maranatha provides the perfect setting for academic discourse and professional networking.

Getting to the Venue

Directions to Maranatha

Universitas Kristen Maranatha is centrally located in Bandung and easily accessible through various transportation options:

From Husein Sastranegara International Airport:

The university is approximately 6 km from the airport. Take a taxi (about 20-30 minutes depending on traffic) or book a ride-hailing service like Grab or Gojek.

From Bandung Railway Station:

The campus is about 4 km from Bandung's main railway station. You can take a taxi, or use ride-hailing services (approximately 15-20 minutes).

From Jakarta via High-Speed Rail (WHOOSH):

Take the WHOOSH high-speed train from Halim Station (Jakarta) to Tegalluar Station (Bandung). From Tegalluar Station to Maranatha, take a taxi or ride-hailing service (approximately 30-40 minutes depending on traffic).

Local Transportation:
  • Taxis: Blue Bird Group, Grab Car
  • Motorcycle taxis: Grab Bike, Gojek
  • Public transportation: Angkot (public minivan) routes passing near the campus
