Registration Process

  1. Download and complete the registration form here
  2. Pay using one of the available methods:
    • Bank Transfer (Wire/TT) - via secure card gateway
    • Indonesian E-Wallets (QRIS) - OVO, GoPay, DANA, LinkAja
    • International Credit Card - for overseas participants
  3. Email the completed form and payment proof to icot2025@ideas-lab.org (include your name and paper ID)
  4. You will receive a confirmation email with your registration details
  5. For onsite participants, collect your conference kit at the registration desk
