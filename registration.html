Important Notes

  • Early bird registration deadline: November 10, 2025
  • Regular registration deadline: November 28, 2025
  • At least one author must register for each accepted paper to be included in the proceedings
  • Registration fee includes access to all sessions, conference materials, coffee breaks, and lunch during conference days
  • For each additional paper, an extra fee of $200 will be charged
  • Each paper is limited to 12 pages. Extra pages (maximum 2) will be charged at $100 per page
  • All payments made are final and non-refundable under any circumstances

Registration Process

  1. Download the registration form here
  2. Fill out the form with your details
  3. Make the payment using one of the available methods
  4. All payments made are final and non-refundable under any circumstances
  5. Email the completed form and payment proof to icot2025@ideas-lab.org
  6. You will receive a confirmation email with your registration details
  7. For onsite participants, collect your conference kit at the registration desk
